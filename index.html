About Us

SM POWER SERVICES was established in the year 2020. The Company is grown as one of the leading organizations in the electrical field and offers a comprehensive range of Services to Electrical Industries. Due to the versatility of our engineering teams, we are able to offer a wide range of services, such as –Electrical Auto CAD drawings, Electrical testing, Programming and Automation, Industrial Electrical Systems Installation, Commissioning, Electrical system Operation & Maintenance, Electrical Energy Audits, Renewable Energy System.
